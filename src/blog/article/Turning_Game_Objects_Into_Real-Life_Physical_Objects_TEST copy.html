Turning Game Objects Into Real-Life Physical Objects

Imagine a device that is capable of taking any virtual game object and turning it into a real-life physical object. Spoiler alert, the device already exists and it's called a 3D printer. However, it is limited to a selection of printable filament, color, and low level of detail.

Let's keep it real and let me continue on how I managed to 3D print a plastic (PLA) miniature from the Qpang game.
